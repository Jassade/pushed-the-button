1. You shall have no other gods before ME.
2. You shall not make FALSE GODS.
3. You shall not take the name of ME in vain.
4. Remember the Sabbath day, to keep it SAFE.
5. Honor NATURE.
6. You shall not BLASPHEME.
7. You shall not LIE.
8. You shall not STEAL.
9. You shall not DEFY.
10. You shall not LIVE.
